Q: Take the last letters of the words in "Elon Musk" and concatenate them.
A: Write down the fisrt word "Elon" in separate letters: "E l o n".
Take the last letter of "E l o n", which is "n".
Write down the Second word "Musk" in separate letters: "M u s k".
Take the last letter of "M u s k", which is "k".
Concatenate the last letters "n" and "k" together.
The concatenation of the last letters "n" and "k" is "n" + "k" = "nk".
Same with the concatenation result "nk", the answer is nk.
